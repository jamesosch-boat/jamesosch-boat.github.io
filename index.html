The Core Proposition

I bridge the gap between creative vision and on-the-ground reality. Operating out of Spain, I specialize in international media production and cross-border strategy—turning complex logistical hurdles into seamless operations.

Whether it means coordinating multilingual teams or adapting to unpredictable environments, my philosophy is straightforward: cut the fluff, build systems that work, and execute without hesitation. I handle the mechanics so you can focus entirely on the mission.

Key Capabilities

My background combines high-level strategic planning with hands-on, high-stakes logistical execution.

  • Cross-Border Project Management: End-to-end execution, budget oversight, and timeline delivery across different countries.
  • International Media Production: Coordinating operations, teams, and logistics for high-end international projects.
  • Multilingual Communication: Fluent in English, German, French, Spanish, and Luxembourgish—bridging cultural and operational gaps in diverse teams.
  • Unconventional Logistics: Managed a remote wilderness lodge and organized dogsled expeditions in the Finnish Arctic.
  • Extreme Adaptability: Proven ability to navigate minimalist, unpredictable environments (including living and operating from a nutshell of a sailboat in the Netherlands and an off-grid base in the Spanish Sierra Nevada).

Selected Cases

A showcase of international media productions where strategic execution directly drove real-world results.

  • The Castle Project

Impact: After two years stagnant on the market with traditional agencies, this targeted cinematic production successfully secured the sale of the estate.

  • The Bründlburger Campaign
